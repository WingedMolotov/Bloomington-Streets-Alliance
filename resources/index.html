    • State and Local Resources

    • Bicycle Alliance of Minnesota (BikeMN) - Statewide bicycle advocates and educational resources and Minnesota's arm of the League of American Bicyclists.

    • Move Minnesota and Move Minnesota Action - Statewide transportation advocates and grassroots organizing.

    • Streets.mn - A statewide land use and transportation blog providing thoughtful perspectives from local writers.

    • MinnPost Cityscape - A column from Saint Paul's Bill Lindeke, offering insights on transportation and development in the Twin Cities.

    • Bike Walk Richfield - Local bicycle and pedestrian advocacy.

    • Edina Bike Alliance - Community-based bicycle advocacy.

    • West Metro Active Transportation Alliance - The BikeMN chapter for St. Louis Park, Hopkins, and Minnetonka.

    • Bike EP - A Facebook group for Eden Prairie advocacy.

    • Our Streets - Statewide advocacy for a safe and accessible transportation system.

    • Saint Paul Bicycle Coalition - Saint Paul's BikeMN chapter.

    • Access Hopkins - Hopkins' BikeMN chapter.

    National Resources

    • League of American Bicyclists - The leading national organization for bicycle advocacy and education.

    • People for Bikes - A national coalition of bicycle industry executives supporting biking and publishing yearly City Ratings.

    • Strong Towns - A national organization focused on changing the North American approach to development and infrastructure.

    Our Principles

    • Safety first. Streets should be safe and comfortable for people of all ages and abilities, regardless of how they choose to get around.

    • Choice. Everyone should have the freedom to walk, bike, take transit, or drive.

    • Community.Smart transportation and thoughtful land use should work together to strengthen neighborhoods, businesses, and public spaces.

    • Fact-based dialogue. We believe respectful dialogue grounded in data and transparency leads to more constructive conversations.
