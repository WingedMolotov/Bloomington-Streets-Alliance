What We Do

The Bloomington Streets Alliance was founded by ordinary people who believe that walking, biking, and transit goes hand-in-hand with creating strong, thriving communities. Sustainable transportation improves health, brings people together, reinforces our small businesses, reduces our climate impact, and makes each day just a little more joyful.

We will achieve these goals through grassroots organization, sharing resources, combating misinformation, and letting the community know where their action can lead to real changes. We believe that all Bloomington residents can benefit from better mobility and land use—but none of this is possible without your support. Please join our movement for a brighter future.
