Bloomington 2050 Comprehensive Plan

Every decade, Bloomington updates its 20-year vision through a community engagement process, setting out the goals, strategies, and actions that guide every aspect of the city, from land use to transportation to housing and climate. Engagement will continue through 2026 and 2027, but it is essential for our voices to be heard early in the process.

Your input can help shape a comprehensive plan that not only identifies the challenges of walking, cycling, and transit in Bloomington, but provides the solutiones needed to create a network of safe routes around the city. Giving people choices in how the get around supports environmental sustainability and community health, as well as economic access and growth, and the vibrancy of our neighborhoods.
